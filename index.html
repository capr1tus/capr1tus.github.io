goals

capr1tus is a group dedicated to providing high quality games, game websites, tools, and other projects that anyone can use, download, or modify as they wish.

our projects focus on:

aesthetic, website feel, and game quality.

any capr1tus project will not contain:

low quality or "default" games found on every other platform, ads, bloat, or anything that makes playing hard to do.
